Welcome to my AI Detection Study

Hello! My name is Lukas Schaffrath, and I study Cognitive, Digital, and Empirical English Studies at RWTH Aachen University. Since I was a teacher trainee for several years beforehand, and the topic of AI-generated texts has become increasingly relevant in education, I became interested in understanding how well university instructors can distinguish between human-written and AI-generated student essays. This study aims to explore this question and gather insights into the detectability of AI in academic writing.

In this study, you will be asked to classify a series of student essays as either AI-generated or human-written. Your task is to rely on your professional judgment and expertise in evaluating written texts.

You will be shown 10 excerpts from BA/MA student essays from RWTH Aachen University: 5 of them are generated by an AI and 5 are human-written. The order of presentation is randomized for each participant.

For each text, please indicate your classification and rate your confidence on a scale from 1 (very uncertain) to 5 (very confident). Take your time to read each text carefully; your response time will also be recorded for analysis.

Your participation is voluntary, and the responses are anonymous except for the basic demographic data you provide.
